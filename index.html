About Me

Hi there! I'm Ariela Lu YE, a Ph.D. student in the linguistics department at Rutgers University. The interface between semantics, pragmatics and syntax remains one of the most intricate challenges in formal linguistics, and is the Everest that I aspire to surmount as part of my long-term career. The topics I am thinking about now are biased questions, wh-conditionals, modal indefinites, conditionals, perspective-sensitive items, and speech verb shuo. I studied at University of Southern California as a Ph.D. student before transferring to Rutgers and I'm also an active member of S-Side Story and Meaning Lab directed by Roumyana Pancheva and Alexis Wellwood respectively.
